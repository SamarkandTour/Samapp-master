How can I get a Uzbekistan VISA?

  • Visit the online VISA application site(http://evisa.mfa.uz/evisa_en/) of Uzbek Ministry of Foreign Affairs and make out a application form. After getting the approval and visit Uzbekistan embassy in your country to get a VISA stamp.

When is the best season for Samarkand tour?

  • Most of time is great except for extremely hot season like July or August.

Can I make a reservation for accommodation using this App?

  • Yes, we're providing a reservation link at the detail screen of each hotel. If you make a reservation by the link, customer doesn't even have any additional reservation fee and it'll also be very helpful to maintain this project continually. Thanks.

Should I have to make a reservation for accommodation in Tashkent before visiting Samarkand

  • It depends on your flight time. If your plane arrives at late night, you would have to make a reservation for the very night. Unfortunately now our App helps you to reserve hotels in Samarkand but we are looking forward to informing you about providing a reservation service of hotels in Tashkent by this App.

Can I make a reservation for railway ticket from Tashkent to Samarkand?

  • There in no way to buy directly the ticket out of Uzbekistan. You may need to utilize a agency to buy a ticket instead of you. For your convenience, now we're finding good way for that service. We are looking forward to informing you of the good news soon.

When is the best time to visit a restaurant?

  • If it's a big restaurant, any time is OK. But if you want to visit one of small local restaurants or restaurants, which has a specialty of one specific food like Osh, we recommend you to visit there before 1 PM because most restaurant could not sell more food since all prepared food for a day have sold out.

Is there any service charge for a served meal in the restaurant?

  • Yes, they will charge you 10~20% of total meal price . Unfortunately you need to know that sometimes like a sightseeing place in other developing country you would meet a owner of restaurant to intend to charge foreigners more much price than local people.

By only GPS, can this App guide me to the destination successfully?

  • Yes, but you need to know that basically a location accuracy by GPS has a aberration about 10m and the aberration can tolerable to find the destination. If there are other factors, you can get more exact signal when sky is clear without clouds and if you have a recent and precise GPS module, accelerate sensor and magnetic sensor in your mobile device, you can get a better result.

What languages are available during tour?

  • Russian language is very popular. At hotels and restaurants prepared for foreigners, english will work well, but it's not easy to get a help on the street in only english.
